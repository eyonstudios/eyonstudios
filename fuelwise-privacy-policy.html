1. Information We Collect

FuelWise does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. All data you enter (routes, fuel prices, vehicle details) is processed locally on your device and is never shared.

2. Google Maps & Location

This app uses Google Maps APIs (Places Autocomplete, Directions, Geocoding) to provide location search and route distance features. When you search for a location or pick a point on the map, your search query or coordinates are sent to Google's servers solely to return results. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Internet Access

The app requires internet access only to communicate with Google Maps APIs. No other network requests are made. We do not run analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs.

4. Data Storage

Your preferences (dark mode setting) are saved locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences storage. This data never leaves your device.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google Maps Platform. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details on how Google handles data sent to their APIs.

6. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app update notes.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's store listing page.
