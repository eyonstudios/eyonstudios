Data Deletion Right

  • As a user, you have the right to request the deletion of your personal data from our systems.
  • Your data deletion right empowers you to ensure that your information is no longer retained by our platform.
  • We respect your privacy, and in accordance with data protection regulations, you can exercise your right to request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time.
  • Upon your request, we will promptly initiate the process to permanently delete your data from our records, ensuring compliance with privacy and data protection standards.
  • Your data deletion right is fundamental to your control over your information; you can request the removal of your account and personal data by contacting our support team or following the specified procedure in our privacy policy.

Instructions for Requesting Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and associated data, please send an email to eyonsupp@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request."

Include your full name, username, and registered email address in the email body for identification purposes.
