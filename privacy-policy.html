This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our application. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to keeping your data secure.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our app:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and username.
  • User Communications: Messages sent in the trade chat are temporarily stored before being automatically deleted.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information such as the model and operating system version for troubleshooting purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate users and allow access to the app.
  • Store user preferences and settings.
  • Improve app functionality and user experience.
  • Monitor and prevent spam or abuse in trade chat.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal data. Your information is stored securely in Firebase Firestore, and messages in the trade chat are automatically deleted.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore Database to manage user data securely. These services have their own privacy policies, which you can review on their official websites.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access & Update: You can update your profile information in the app settings.
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account by going to your profile.
  • Opt-Out: You can choose to stop using the app at any time.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app, and we encourage you to review the policy periodically.
